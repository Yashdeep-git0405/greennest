Our Story

GreenNest was founded by Yashdeep with one simple mission — to make fresh, high-quality groceries accessible to every household in India. Starting from Thanesar, Haryana, we've grown into a trusted brand that hundreds of families rely on every day.

We believe that what you eat matters. That's why we work directly with local farmers, cut out middlemen, and deliver produce that is picked fresh every single morning. Every item you see on GreenNest is quality-checked before it reaches your door.
