How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order is placed, you'll receive an SMS with a tracking link. You can also check "My Orders" in your account to see live delivery status.
What is the return & refund policy? ▼
We offer 100% refund or replacement for damaged, expired, or wrong items. Raise a complaint within 24 hours of delivery with a photo, and we'll process your request within 2 business days.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 2–4 hours. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM. Express delivery (within 90 minutes) is available in select areas.
Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 15 minutes of placing them. After that, the order goes into processing. Please contact support immediately if needed.
Are the products sourced fresh daily? ▼
Yes! GreenNest sources fruits, vegetables, and dairy products daily from local farms and verified suppliers to ensure maximum freshness in every delivery.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. All online transactions are secured with 256-bit encryption.
